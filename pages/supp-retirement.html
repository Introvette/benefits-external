Supplemental Retirement Plans

Supplemental retirement income is the amount of income a retiree earns on top of the income he receives from Social Security and pension payments. Eligible employees can save for retirement by making pre-tax contributions to a 401(k), 403(b), or 457 plan or after-tax contributions to a Roth 403(b) or Roth 457 plan.

OMNI is Charlotte Mecklenburg schools' Third Party Administrator (TPA) for all 403b and 457 plans. OMNI provides administrative services (such as salary reduction agreements, loans, roll-overs, etc.) to help manage our plans with vendors. If you have any questions, please call OMNI online at www.omni403b.com or call Customer Service Toll Free: (877) 544-6664.
