Leave of Absence

Charlotte Mecklenburg values your contributions to helping every child every day. At some point during your career with Charlotte Mecklenburg School, you may find it necessary to request a leave of absence from your position. This website provides detailed information about Leaves of Absence (LOA) to help you understand the proper course of action when requesting a LOA. It is very important that you talk with your supervisor regarding a LOA request as well as contacting a Human Resource Leave Coordinator.

How to apply for a leave of absence:

Option 1

  1. Login to the Employee Intranet
  2. Click on Leave Request Form
  3. Complete the Leave request form by entering start and return date and reason for leave.
  4. Upload required documentation and click SAVE. (WH-380F form or other supporting documentation)
  5. After you click save, you and your supervisor will receive a confirmation email stating that your request has been received. If additional information is needed, you will receive an email from the Benefits Department.

Option 2

  1. Click on the Request for Leave of Absence (under Leave of Absence forms)
