Workers' Compensation

Workers' compensation or workers' comp is a form of insurance providing wage replacement and medical benefits to employees injured in the course of employment.

If you are hurt on the job, please complete the forms below and email: workerscompensation@cms.k12.nc.us

Workers' Compensation Summary: Any employee that is injured on the job qualifies for Workers' Compensation (WC). The length of a WC leave is determined by the injury. WC runs concurrent with FMLA and other leaves as applicable in a rolling 12-month period.
