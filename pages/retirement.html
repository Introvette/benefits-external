State Retirement System

Provider: NC Department of State Treasurer

Member Service: 919-814-4590

Website: www.nctreasurer.com

Permanent full-time employees are covered by a defined plan with the North Carolina Teacher's and State Employees' Retirement System. Employees are required to contribute 6% of their monthly salary through a payroll deduction. Charlotte Mecklenburg Schools also contributes on your behalf to the State Retirement System in an amount that is legislated annually. Your contributions are paid with pre-tax dollars, thereby reducing current state and federal income taxes.

To make an appointment for retirement planning or signing, please click here.

New Employees hired after January 1, 2021, or After are NOT Eligible for Retiree Medical Benefits

Employees hired on or after January 1, 2021, who enroll in the Teacher's and State Employees' Retirement System (TSERS) will not be able to continue health insurance coverage under the North Carolina State Health Plan when they retire. If you have any questions, please contact the NC TSERS program at 919-814-4590.



Retirees will be auto-enrolled into a health plan regardless of their contribution status. If they do not want coverage, it is necessary to opt out during the retirement process by calling 855-859-0966 or going online. Depending on the retiree's situation at the time of retirement, they will need to take this into consideration regarding their State Health Plan coverage.

You may retire with full benefits at:

  • Age sixty-five (65) with at least five (5) years of contributory service
  • Age sixty (60) with twenty-five (25) years of service; or
  • Anytime after you have thirty (30) years of service credit regardless of age

You may take early retirement at a reduced monthly payment:

  • At age fifty (50) with at least twenty (20) years of service credit
  • At age sixty (60) with at least five (5) years of contributory service.

For more detailed information, please visit MY NC Retirement where you can download the current Retirement Handbook.

You may also access ORBIT (Online Retirement Benefit through Integrated Technology) to manage your retirement account and gain access to the full features of ORBIT.
